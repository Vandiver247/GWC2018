Hindi USA

Hindi USA has a total of 21 schools, with 15 in New Jersey, 4 in Connecticut, and 1 in both Maryland & Massachusetts. It is one of the largest Hindi volunteer groups in America consisting of over 4,000 members. Hindi USA is a non-profit and a completely volunteer based organization in the US.
