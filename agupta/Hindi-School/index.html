Hindi USA

Hindi USA has a total of 21 schools, with 15 in New Jersey, 4 in Connecticut, and 1 in both Maryland & Massachusetts. It is one of the largest Hindi volunteer groups in America consisting of over 4,000 members. Hindi USA is a non-profit and a completely volunteer based organization in the US.

Basking Ridge Hindi School


Dr. Sanjay Gupta established the Basking Ridge Hindi School in September, 2017. In our first year we accumulated over 60 students which was the highest enrollment record to date. We have 12 volunteers for our classes and 3 youth volunteers, Two of the youth volunteers are in RHS and one in WAMS.
